01 / SALES

SALES

Reciprocation & the attorney relationship most clinics never build.

Sales in PI isn't ads — it's the in-person reciprocation game with attorneys and paralegals that almost no chiro is running because they think it's overhead. It doesn't have to be a full-time role. One day a week of relationship-building plus a small marketing-automation layer keeps you top of mind for every PI attorney in your market.

  • The one-day-a-week sales role: who, where, what they do
  • Visiting law offices and building paralegal relationships
  • $500/mo attorney-targeted social to stay top of mind
  • The reciprocation framework that flips the referral dynamic
02 / CARE

CARE & CASE VALUE

Why the same patient becomes a $3K bill at one clinic and a $10K bill at another.

If Michael Jordan got rear-ended, what kind of care would he get? That's the standard a PI patient legitimately deserves — because the insurance is paying, not the patient. Higher case value isn't a markup; it's better tools, better protocols, and better documentation. And attorneys route every new case to the higher-value clinic.

  • The Michael Jordan standard of care, applied to every PI patient
  • $50 retraction vs. $500 decompression — when each is justified
  • Protocols and documentation that hold up under adjuster review
  • How attorney economics (1/3 / 1/3 / 1/3) reward higher case value
03 / CASE MGMT

CASE MANAGEMENT

Do the attorney's job for them — become the only clinic they call.

Attorneys don't refer to the best clinic — they refer to the easiest one to work with. Case management is doing the parts of the attorney's job they hate doing themselves: HIPAA on day one, clear treatment plans, medical records on demand, regular case updates. Nail it and you become the default clinic.

  • The week-one checklist: HIPAA, treatment plan, intake docs
  • Records and reporting cadence that attorneys actually use
  • What "important to the case" means — and how to manage it
  • The two-way pipeline: you make their job easier, they send the next case

THE FLYWHEEL

Your digital arm doesn't just generate leads. It feeds the flywheel.

SEO, PPC, and paid social bring in the unattached patient — the highest-leverage patient in PI, because they walk in without an attorney attached. Treat them well, manage their case cleanly, and they become a two-way referral: back to your clinic, and forward to the attorney who handles their case.
